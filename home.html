Meet Jesugbogo

I am a data scientist on a quest to change the world. Well, maybe not in that sense, but you get the point. I believe that words and data are the two most powerful tools to change the world and I love both. I am inquisitive, analytical and love to pay attention to detail. This led to my passion for data science. My skills include data analysis, visualization, and machine learning. I have developed a strong acumen for problem solving, and I enjoy an occasional challenge. I often work on end-to-end data science projects that usually begin from collecting data from third party sources and end with delivering business insights. I also love writing so I take time to write articles and publish them on the Internet. I also enjoy going on sites like HackerRank and trying out their programming challenges. I am seeking opportunities to gain more skills, hone the ones I already possess in order to build a successful career in Data Science and Analytics. You can take a look at some of my projects and articles in the section below. I will link my work to their GitHub repositories, so feel free to download my code and play around with it. Most of my education has come from online platforms. I got in for the Women Techsters Fellowship to study Data Science. I have also downloaded e-books, audited courses on Coursera and Datacamp and spent countless hours on YouTube learning. I am grateful to online educators who have given me the opportunity to learn these things, and for democratizing education.
